# 🍗 Chicken Nuggets Health Benefits Website

A fun and informative website dedicated to exploring the health benefits of chicken nuggets.

## Overview

This website educates visitors about the nutritional value and health benefits of chicken nuggets, breaking down common misconceptions and providing practical information for health-conscious consumers.

## Features

- **Comprehensive Benefits Guide**: Learn about 6 major health benefits including protein content, energy boost, bone health, brain function, heart health, and convenience.

- **Detailed Nutrition Information**: View a complete nutritional breakdown per 100g serving, including key nutrients like B12, niacin, selenium, phosphorus, zinc, and iron.

- **Healthy Recipes**: Discover 4 creative and nutritious ways to incorporate chicken nuggets into your diet:
  - Crispy Salad Bowl
  - Whole Wheat Wrap
  - Veggie Stir-Fry
  - Pasta Primavera

- **FAQ Section**: Get answers to common questions about chicken nugget nutrition, cooking methods, and dietary considerations.

- **Responsive Design**: Fully responsive website that works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

## Website Sections

1. **Hero Section**: Eye-catching introduction to the topic
2. **Benefits Section**: 6-card grid showcasing key health benefits
3. **Nutrition Section**: Detailed nutritional breakdown and key nutrients
4. **Recipes Section**: 4 healthy recipe ideas with benefits explained
5. **FAQ Section**: 6 frequently asked questions and answers
6. **Conclusion Section**: Summary and dietary recommendations
